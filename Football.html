Introduction to Football

Football is a team sport played between two teams of eleven players. The main objective is to score goals by kicking the ball into the opponent’s goal. Football improves physical fitness, teamwork, and leadership skills.

Basic Rules of Football

  • Each team has 11 players
  • The match is played for 90 minutes
  • Hands are not allowed (except goalkeeper)
  • The team with more goals wins

Equipment Used

  • Football
  • Football boots
  • Shin guards
  • Goalposts
  • Team jersey

Benefits of Football

  • Improves stamina and strength
  • Develops teamwork and discipline
  • Enhances speed and coordination
  • Boosts mental health

Career Options in Football

Football offers many career opportunities such as professional player, coach, referee, sports trainer, and sports analyst.
